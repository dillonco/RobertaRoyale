Rules of Euchre

The Deck

24 cards: 9, 10, J, Q, K, A in all four suits. Four players in two partnerships (North–South vs East–West). Each player gets 5 cards.

Trump & Bowers

  • Right Bower — Jack of the trump suit (highest trump).
  • Left Bower — Jack of the same-color suit as trump (second highest trump, counts as trump).
  • Color pairs: ♥ Hearts ↔ ♦ Diamonds  ·  ♠ Spades ↔ ♣ Clubs
  • Trump order (high→low): Right Bower, Left Bower, A, K, Q, 10, 9

Bidding — Round 1

The dealer turns up a card (the "up card"). Starting left of dealer, each player may order it up (accepting that suit as trump — dealer picks up the card and discards) or pass. When ordering up, a player may also go alone (partner sits out). The dealer says "pick it up" or "turn it down."

Bidding — Round 2

If all four pass in round 1, each player may name any suit as trump (except the turned-down suit) or pass. The dealer is stuck — they must name a suit if all others pass.

Playing Tricks

  • You must follow suit if you can. The Left Bower follows trump, not its printed suit.
  • Highest trump wins. If no trump is played, highest card of the led suit wins.
  • Trick winner leads the next trick.

Scoring

  • Makers take 3–4 tricks → 1 point
  • Makers take all 5 (march) → 2 points
  • Lone player takes all 5 → 4 points
  • Makers fail to take 3 (euchred) → opponents get 2 points

First team to reach the target score (5, 7, or 10) wins.
